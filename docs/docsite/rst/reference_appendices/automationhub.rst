Assible Automation Hub
======================

`Assible Automation Hub <https://www.assible.com/products/automation-hub>`_ is the official location to discover and download supported :ref:`collections <collections>`, included as part of an Assible Automation Platform subscription. These content collections contain modules, plugins, roles, and playbooks in a downloadable package.

Assible Automation Hub gives you direct access to trusted content collections from Red Hat and Certified Partners. You can find content by topic or Assible Partner organizations.

Assible Automation Hub is the downstream Red Hat supported product version of Assible Galaxy. Find out more about Assible Automation Hub features and how to access it at `Assible Automation Hub <https://www.assible.com/products/automation-hub>`_. Assible Automation Hub is part of the Red Hat Assible Automation Platform subscription, and comes bundled with support from Red Hat, Inc.
